## Idea 1: The "Obsidian" Minimalist (Chosen)
**Design Movement**: High-End Utility / Obsidian-esque
**Core Principles**: 
- Information Density with Clarity
- Stealth Aesthetics (Deep Blacks & Subtle Grays)
- Focus on Content (Screenshots as the Hero)
- Zero Friction Navigation
**Color Philosophy**: 
- Background: `oklch(0.12 0.01 285)` (Deep Slate/Black)
- Accents: `oklch(0.65 0.15 250)` (Electric Blue for active states)
- Text: `oklch(0.95 0.01 285)` (Off-white for readability)
**Layout Paradigm**: 
- Fixed Sidebar for Search & Filters
- Horizontal Scrollable Timeline at the bottom
- Central Stage for the "Current Moment" screenshot
**Signature Elements**: 
- Glassmorphism overlays for the menu bar app feel
- Micro-app icons with subtle glows
- "Time-scrubber" with tactile feedback feel
**Interaction Philosophy**: 
- Search-first: Instant results as you type
- Scrubbing: Smooth, high-frame-rate timeline navigation
**Animation**: 
- Spring-based transitions for screenshot pops
- Staggered entry for search results
**Typography System**: 
- Display: "Geist Sans" or "Inter" (Bold, tight tracking)
- Body: "Geist Mono" for timestamps and metadata
